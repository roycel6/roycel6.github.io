About Me

but eye-catching designs. Languages I have used in the past include HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and PHP. Frameworks I've used

working together and combining different ideas is the best and easiest way to solve a problem. I excel at learning new languages and frameworks
quickly, and try to develop each project differently from the last.
