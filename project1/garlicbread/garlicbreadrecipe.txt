		<img src="meltbutter.jpg" alt="butter"> </img> 4 Tablespoons of Melted Butter

List of Steps -
Step 1 Proof the Yeast
		 <p>Take 1/2 cup of warm water to the bowl of a stand mixer fitted with a dough hook.</p> <button>Add Water</button>  
		  <p>Sprinkle sugar and yeast into the water. Let this stand for 5 to 10 minutes until it is foamy.</p> <button>Add Yeast</button> <button id="addsugar"> Add Sugar</button>
		  <p>Let this stand for 5 to 10 minutes until it is foamy. </p><button>Activate Yeast</button>
Step 2 Make the Dough
          <p>Mix in butter, milk, and stir flour in gradually.</p> 
		  <button>Add Butter</button>
		  <button>Add Milk</button> 
		  <button>Add Flour</button>
Step 3 Knead
			<p>Knead for 7 to 10 minutes, until the dough is smooth with stretch.</p> 
			<a href="#knead-container" class="action-bttn">KNEAD</a>
Step 4 Make the Topping
          <p>Using a small bowl, combine the melted butter, garlic, salt, and parsley.</p> 
		  <button>Add Melted Butter</button>
		  <button>Add Garlic</button> 
		  <button>Add Salt</button> 
		  <button>Add Parsley</button>
Step 5 Make the Loaf
				<p>Divide dough into round parts and dip the dough balls into the topping, arranging into a loaf.</p> 
				<a href="#dough-container" class="action-bttn">DIVIDE</a> 
				<a href="#loaf-container" class="action-bttn">ARRANGE</a>
				<br> <br>
				<p>Cover and let rise until doubled, about 1 hour. Reserve the leftover butter mixture.</p> <a href="#loaf-container" class="action-bttn">RISE</a><br><br><br>
				<a href="#loaf-container" class="action-bttn">STEP 6: BAKE THE BREAD</a>
Step 6 Bake the Bread
			<p>Bake at 350°F (176°C) for 30 minutes or until golden brown.</p>
			<a href="#uncook-container" class="action-bttn">BAKE</a>
			<p>Paste the butter mixture on the pull-apart garlic bread after they are out of the oven.</p>
			<a href="#cook-container" class="action-bttn">FINISH</a>
