Step 2: Brew the Tea

STIR in 2 tablespoons of sugar, mix until completely dissolved.

GRAB a lemon and CUT it into thin slices. Put 2-3 slices inside the mason jar and one on the glass rim.

GRAB a handful of ice cubes and put into the mason jar. This will cool it. PUT the a straw in the mason jar and stir. Now, enjoy your Sweet Tea!
